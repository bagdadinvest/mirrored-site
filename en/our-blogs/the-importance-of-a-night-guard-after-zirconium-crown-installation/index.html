The Importance of a Night Guard After Zirconium Crown Installation

DentiDelil Editorials / Sept. 22, 2024 / Branding

What is a Night Guard?

A night guard is a custom-made dental appliance worn over the teeth to prevent grinding (bruxism) or clenching, particularly during sleep. This device acts as a protective barrier between the upper and lower teeth, absorbing the pressure and preventing damage to the crowns or natural teeth.

Why a Night Guard is Essential After Zirconium Installation

1. Protects Against Teeth Grinding

Teeth grinding is a common problem, often occurring unconsciously during sleep. Over time, this habit can wear down zirconium crowns, even though zirconium is a tough material. A night guard provides a protective layer that minimizes direct contact between teeth, reducing the risk of grinding-induced damage to your crowns.

2. Prevents Excessive Pressure on Crowns

Zirconium crowns, while incredibly strong, are not indestructible. When you clench your teeth, it can place significant pressure on the crowns. This excessive force could eventually lead to cracks or fractures in the crowns. A night guard helps to distribute the pressure evenly across your teeth, protecting the crowns from undue stress.

3. Extends the Lifespan of Your Dental Work

Investing in a night guard can prolong the life of your zirconium crowns. By reducing the impact of grinding and clenching, the guard ensures that your crowns remain in optimal condition for many years. Without a night guard, the constant wear and tear on the crowns could result in the need for costly repairs or replacements sooner than expected.
