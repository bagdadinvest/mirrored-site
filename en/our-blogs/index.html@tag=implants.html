The Importance of a Night Guard After Zirconium Crown Installation

After receiving zirconium crowns, many patients are advised to wear a night guard to protect their new dental restorations. Zirconium crowns are known for their strength and durability, but like all dental work, they still require proper care and protection, especially during nighttime when teeth grinding and clenching can occur. In this blog, we will explore the importance of wearing a night guard after zirconium installation and how it can extend the life of your dental restorations.
